# Product Overview

## Product
**Name**: Simple CRM *(name to be confirmed during onboarding)*
**Category**: Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
**Stage**: MVP concept

## What It Is
A lightweight, personal CRM for individuals and small teams. Three core jobs:
1. **Track contacts** — a centralized contact book with key details per person
2. **Log interactions** — attach timestamped notes, calls, emails, and meetings to any contact
3. **View the pipeline** — a visual view of deals/opportunities organized by stage

## Users
Solo operators, freelancers, consultants, and small sales teams who need organized relationship management without the complexity or cost of enterprise CRM software.

## Positioning
Simple and fast. Not Salesforce. A tool that stays out of your way and keeps your relationships and pipeline visible at a glance.

## Tone & Voice
Practical, direct, no jargon. Focused on outcomes, not features.

## Core Scope (MVP)
1. **Contact management** — add, view, edit, and search contacts
2. **Interaction logging** — record interactions per contact with type (call / email / meeting / note), timestamp, and free-text notes
3. **Pipeline view** — deal stages (Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Closed Won / Lost)

## Strategic Principles
- Simplicity over feature-richness
- Speed of entry: logging an interaction should take under 30 seconds
- Pipeline must be immediately scannable — no drilling required
- Zero onboarding friction: up and useful in minutes
